Budget Pack

Includes Arduino Uno, 3' USB cable, Half-sized Breadboard, 75 breadboard wires, 1k & 10k Potentiometer, 2 small pushbuttons, 5 bright LED's, 1 RGB Ultra-bright LED, 5 100 ohm resistors, 5 1k resistors, 5 10k resistors, and a light sensor.

Experimentation Kit

13 circuits to explore microcontroller basics. Covering LEDs, transistors, motors, integrated circuits, pushbuttons, variable resistors, photo resistors, temperature sensors & relays.
